One Piece

Started: July 22, 1997

"One Piece" is a Japanese manga and anime series created by Eiichiro Oda. It follows the journey of Monkey D. Luffy, a young pirate with the ability to stretch his body like rubber after eating a Devil Fruit. Luffy sets out on a grand adventure to find the legendary treasure known as the "One Piece" and become the King of the Pirates. Accompanied by his diverse crew of Straw Hat Pirates, including swordsman Roronoa Zoro, navigator Nami, sharpshooter Usopp, and musician Brook, Luffy faces numerous challenges, fights powerful enemies, and explores new islands and cultures. The series is renowned for its intricate world-building, deep character development, and themes of friendship, adventure, and pursuit of dreams.
